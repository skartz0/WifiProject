Cape Drepano Surf House

If you want a really unique kite experience, this is the trip for you! We will guide to the best kitespots according to the weather, to popular kitespots like Cape Drepano, flat lagoons, river mouths, do amazing downwinds, and explore uncharted kite spots. We will set our base camp at “Cape Drepano Surf House” and every day we will chase the best conditions for kitesurfing according to your skills and we can guarantee 90% wind for your trip!
